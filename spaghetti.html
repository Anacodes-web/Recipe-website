Spaghetti-Fiesta

Spaghetti Fiesta: A creative Nigerian dish that combines spaghetti with a rich and flavorful veggies and coconut milk based sauce, infused with aromatic spices and herbs, resulting in a unique and delicious culinary experience.

  • preptime: 20mins
  • cooktime: 25mins
  • Total Time: 45mins
  • servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g cooked spaghetti
  • 1 red bell pepper(cut in strips)
  • 1 green bell pepper (cut in strips)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (cut in strips)
  • 1 onion bulb (cut in strips)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 spring onions (cut in strips)
  • 5-8 large fresh tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3 scotch bonnet peppers(chopped)
  • 1/ 2 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1/ 4 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/ 2 tablespoon ginger powder
  • 1/ 2 tablespoon paprika powder
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cooking spoon vegetable oil
  • salt to taste

INGREDIENTS:FOR THE CHICKEN

  • 2 chicken breasts (cut in bite size pieces)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cummin powder
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

PROCEDURE

  1. Place the chicken pieces and all ingredients under chicken in a mixing bowl, mix to combine. You can leave to marinade for at least one hour or go ahead and stir-fry immediately.
  2. Place a pan on medium heat and add a drizzle of oil.
  3. Then add the chicken pieces and stir-fry for about 4-5 minutes or until the chicken is slightly brown. Take out of the pan and set aside.
  4. Place a pot on medium heat and add the vegetable oil. Allow to heat up for a few minutes before adding the onions, garlic and scotch bonnet pepper. Stir-fry till fragrant.
  5. Afterwards, add the curry powder, thyme, seasoning cubes, ground ginger, paprika powder and salt to taste. Stir-fry for another 1 minute.
  6. Next add the chopped tomatoes, stir to combine. Leave to simmer down for 5-7 minutes or until the water content has dried out.
  7. Then add the bell peppers and stir-fry for 2 minutes before adding the coconut milk and cooked chicken. Leave to simmer for 5-7 minutes or until the liquid is reduced by half.
  8. Finally, add the cooked spaghetti, mix to combine. Leave to cook for a final 2 minutes, then take off the heat immediately.
  9. Garnish with some fresh herbs and serve
