Chicken Burger

This chicken burger is a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that is perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. The chicken is marinated in a blend of spices and then pan-seared to perfection. It is then sandwiched between two soft bread buns with lettuce, tomato, and cheese. The result is a juicy and flavorful burger that will satisfy your cravings. Serve with fries or a side salad for a complete meal.

  • preptime: 20mins
  • cooktime: 10mins
  • Total Time: 30mins
  • servings: 3

INGREDIENTS:FOR THE CHICKEN

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced scotch bonnet pepper (atarado)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cummin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 1/2teaspoon ginger powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • Salt to taste

INGREDIENTS:FOR THE BURGER

  • 3 bread rolls
  • 9 tomato slices
  • 3 cheese slices
  • 3 pieces cooked checken
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup

PROCEDURE

  1. pat the chicken dry and cut in half. Make sure the chicken is as flat as possible.
  2. Add all ingredients under chicken into a mixing bowl, including the chicken. Mix thoroughly to combine and leave to marinate for at least 1hr in the fridge
  3. After that drizzle some oil in a pan placed on medium to high heat. When the oil is hot, place the chicken and pan sear on both sides for 4-5mins or until completely browned or cooked.

    4. NOTE: Chicken breast does not require a long time to cook. But if the chicken pieces don't cook through after pan-searing, reduce the heat to the lowest, cover the pan and allow to cook for another 5mins. This will definitely cook it through.

  4. To assemble the burger, cut each bread bun in half, mix the mayonnaise and ketchup in a small mixing bowl and generously coat each bread bun with the mayo and ketchup mixture.
  5. Then place one bun on the flat surface and top with one lettuce leaf, followed by the cooked chicken,some tomato slices and a cheese slice. Top finally with another bread bun and set aside.
  6. Assemble the remaining ingredients to form more chicken burger
